Click on the image to see the program in action!

TASK

For my final project in my Interactive Design and Algorithmic Problem Solving II class, I was instructed to combine concepts that we learned that semester to create "something" interactive.

SOLUTION

I decided to create a program that simulated a phone screen, except when the user hovers over the icon, the pixels of the icon get replaced with a bunch of words to create the icon. When clicked, the words break apart to reveal the home screen of the app. In order to get back to the home screen, the user just has to click and drag upwards to simulate the swiping away of the app.

Technology used: Javascript, p5.js, p5.DOM
