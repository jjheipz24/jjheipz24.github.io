Suicide Awareness Posters

TASK

The goal of this assignment was to choose a social or political issue and create three different and distinct poster designs campaigning for whichever issue chosen. Each poster design needed to incorporate a source link and some kind of "call-to-action."

SOLUTION

The issue I decided to cover was suicide awareness, specifically in teens. This topic means a lot to me on a personal level, so I knew I could create designs that left an emotional impact as well.

The first design (and concept I had) was to depict a noose composed of hateful sayings people may get thrown at them. The second design played with perspective to show the outline of a body like you see at crime scenes, which implies someone jumped off the top of a building. My final design is meant to resemble a suicide note, while there is the shadow of what looks like a child about to shoot themselves shown on the note.

I wanted to create posters that make people feel. Suicide is a very serious topic, and even though my designs may seem dark, I feel they are very fitting for the issue.

Technology used: Adobe Illustrator
