At MassMutual, I spent my summer working on the user interface for their Finance Data and Analytics Portal. The existing interface was outdated and hard to navigate, so it was my job to make it more responsive and more user-friendly.

I was originally assigned to the SAP Development team, but with my skills primarily being in front-end development, I ultimately worked alongside and under the mentorship of an employee of MassMutual who worked on the portal.

Coming into this internship, I had only ever used the Vue framework. This DNA portal used Angular, so over the course of about 2 weeks, I taught myself Angular in order to combine it with my existing HTML, CSS, and Javascript knowledge.
